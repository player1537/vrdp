VRDP

Virtual Reality Remote Desktop Viewer

This project intends to offer an easy way to test out a VR-style of remote desktop application, or in other words, a way to view your computer screen in VR. This may not seem immediately useful, but it would also allow working with your computer in whatever environment you want, such as in the woods or at a beach.

Due to restrictions of WebRTC and WebVR, there are several steps that need to be done to get the system started. This assumes you're using a laptop for the screen and a phone for your VR device.

  1. Laptop: Click the button below to create a new room.
  2. Phone: Scan the QR code with your WebVR device to connect to your computer.
  3. Laptop: Select what screen(s) you want to view from the phone.
  4. Phone: Press the "Start Video" button.
  5. Phone: Press the "Enter VR" button.
  6. Phone: Put your phone in whatever VR viewer you use.
  7. Laptop: Use your computer like normal, but now see your screen in VR.
  8. Laptop: Using the "controller" page, you can change different things like the scale of the displays, how the screens are split, and recenter the displays using the box on the screen.

Note: Though the ideal use case is to position the screens in some 360-degree environment, I haven't done this yet. This is mostly a tech demo to see if the idea is even feasible.
